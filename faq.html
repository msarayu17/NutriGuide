Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use the features? - An account is required to save your plans, track progress, and access personalized features.

How do I use NutriNest? - Enter details, get your plan.

Is calorie tracking accurate? - Yes, data is verified.

Do you support vegetarian or non-veg diets? - Yes, we offer multiple diet types including vegetarian, vegan, and non-vegetarian.

Is my data safe? - Your information is securely stored and never shared with third parties.

I have a health condition. Can I follow the plans? - You can use NutriNest, but we recommend consulting a doctor for medical-specific diets.

How can I contact support? - You can reach us through the Contact page by submitting your name, email, phone number, and feedback.
