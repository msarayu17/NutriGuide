Diet Plans

Weight Loss Diet

Overview: Low-calorie, nutrient-dense meals that promote fat loss.

Who It's For: Anyone wanting to lose weight safely.

Key Features: High protein, high fibre, low sugar.

Diet Chart

Breakfast: Oats + fruits, 1 boiled egg, green tea.

Lunch: 1–2 chapati/brown rice, dal, sabzi, salad.

Snacks: Fruit + green tea.

Dinner: Grilled chicken/paneer + soup + salad.

Weight Gain Diet

Overview: High-calorie, nutrient-rich meals for healthy weight gain.

Who It's For: People who want to gain healthy weight.

Key Features: Healthy fats, frequent meals, high carbs.

Diet Chart

Breakfast: Peanut butter sandwich, banana, milk.

Lunch: 2–3 chapati/rice with chicken or paneer.

Snacks: Nuts, milkshakes, protein shake.

Dinner: Egg/paneer curry + rice/chapati + curd.

Balanced Diet

Overview: Provides all essential nutrients in the right proportions.

Who It's For: Suitable for everyone.

Key Features: Includes all food groups, portion-controlled.

Diet Chart

Breakfast: Idli/dosa + fruit + milk.

Lunch: 2 chapati, dal, veg curry, curd.

Snacks: Nuts, fruit, coconut water.

Dinner: Veg khichdi or pulao + soup.

Muscle Gain Diet

Overview: High-protein meals to support muscle building and recovery.

Who It's For: Gym-goers and athletes.

Key Features: High protein, complex carbs, healthy fats.

Diet Chart

Breakfast: 4-egg omelette/paneer, oats, coffee.

Lunch: Chicken/tofu + brown rice + dal.

Snacks: Protein shake + banana.

Dinner: Fish/paneer + quinoa/sweet potato + veggies.

Vegetarian Diet

Overview: Plant-based diet with dairy and vegetarian protein sources.

Who It's For: People who avoid meat but eat dairy.

Key Features: High fibre, plant proteins, low fat.

Diet Chart

Breakfast: Poha/upma + fruit + tea.

Lunch: 2 chapati, dal, sabzi, paneer.

Snacks: Nuts, smoothie, juice.

Dinner: Veg pulao + curd + salad.

Vegan Diet

Overview: 100% plant-based meals with no animal products.

Who It's For: Vegans and lactose-intolerant individuals.

Key Features: Plant proteins, B12 & iron focus.

Diet Chart

Breakfast: Almond milk oats + fruit + coffee.

Lunch: Quinoa + mixed beans + stir-fry veggies.

Snacks: Nuts, seeds, vegan smoothie.

Dinner: Tofu curry + brown rice + salad.
